Team

Engineering roles

The team is composed by role. The exact setup depends on the task: consulting, prototype development, implementation, testing, or documentation-heavy delivery.

Hardware Engineer

Schematics, PCB inputs, connectors, signal interfaces, power domains, bring-up, and measurement support.

Mechanical Engineer

Enclosures, brackets, fixtures, cable routing, mounting concepts, CAD support, and assembly constraints.

Firmware Engineer

MCU firmware, drivers, communication protocols, diagnostics, hardware abstraction, and test utilities.

ROS 2 / Model-Based Engineer

ROS 2 integration, control nodes, MATLAB/Simulink models, simulation, and embedded deployment support.

Manual QA Engineer

Test cases, acceptance checks, issue reproduction, test evidence, and structured reports.

Process Support

Task tracking, document flow, procurement follow-up, meeting notes, and delivery coordination.
