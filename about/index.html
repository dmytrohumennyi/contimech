About ContiMech

ContiMech (Continuous Mechatronics) is an interdisciplinary engineering startup founded in 2025 in Kyiv. We unite specialists with strong academic and industrial backgrounds in robotics, automotive, and embedded systems.

Who We Are

We are a team of engineers formed through collaboration in multiple commercial projects. Our foundation combines academic roots with industrial precision. We emphasize structured engineering, documentation, and model-based development as integral parts of every process.

Principles

  • System thinking. Components form measurable, goal-oriented systems.
  • Process integrity. Measurable, repeatable, auditable flow from concept to verification.
  • Abstraction first. Architecture and models precede construction or code.
  • Controlled adaptability. Scalable, reconfigurable, failure-tolerant designs.
  • Verification by design. Validation starts at requirements and persists through release.
  • Transparent communication. Interfaces are explicit, documented, traceable.
