Case-study format

Each project here is presented as a short description plus a PDF artifact (presentation, extract, or one-pager). Clicking a tile opens the corresponding PDF. This keeps the page stable while the supporting materials can be updated independently.

  • PDF location: assets/cases/
  • Naming: case_<project_id>.pdf (for example, case_sci_0123U103640.pdf)
  • Auto-linking: if projects.json contains a PDF link, the button appears automatically.

NDA note

Commercial cases are intentionally generalized to avoid exposing customers to risk and to stay within NDA boundaries. If more detail is possible in your context, contact me and I will share what can be disclosed.
