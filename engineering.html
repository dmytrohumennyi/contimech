Flagship Case: ECU Switching System

A comprehensive hardware–software system that automates switching between multiple ECUs for a Tier-1 automotive supplier. The system integrates CAN, JTAG/XCP/XETK connectivity, thermal and load testing, and HIL automation.

  • Multi-ECU switching and signal routing.
  • USB and CAN control protocol with presence detection.
  • Hardware test plan and A/B/C sample phases.
